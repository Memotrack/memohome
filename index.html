What is MemoTrack?

MemoTrack is a digital workflow management platform designed for hospitals and healthcare facilities. It helps staff report infrastructure problems, track maintenance tasks, and monitor issue resolution through a centralized system.

Hospitals manage complex infrastructure including medical equipment, electrical systems, sanitation facilities, and operational processes. MemoTrack helps streamline these activities by providing a structured issue tracking system.

Key Platform Features

Issue Reporting

Staff can quickly report infrastructure or equipment issues through a simple digital interface.

Task Assignment

Reported problems are assigned to the responsible maintenance or administrative teams.

Real-Time Tracking

Administrators can monitor progress and track the status of all maintenance requests.

Administrative Dashboard

Centralized dashboards provide insights into facility performance and operational efficiency.

Common Hospital Workflow Problems

Many hospitals still rely on informal communication methods such as phone calls, manual registers, or verbal reporting to report facility issues. These approaches often lead to delays, lost requests, and inefficient maintenance management.

Without a structured tracking system, administrators may struggle to monitor which issues have been resolved and which are still pending. MemoTrack solves this challenge by providing a transparent and trackable digital workflow system.

Benefits of Digital Issue Tracking

Faster Response Time

Maintenance teams receive instant notifications when issues are reported.

Improved Communication

Departments can communicate more effectively through a centralized system.

Operational Transparency

Administrators gain better visibility into facility operations.

Better Facility Management

Hospitals can maintain infrastructure more efficiently and prevent major issues.
